About Us

Who Am I?

I'm studying computer engineering at Çukurova University and I'm in my 3rd year.
My interest in reading books started at a very young age. While determining a purpose for the project, my interest in the books was very helpful in determining this purpose. My goal in this project is to create a website where people can write summaries about the book they are reading and to encourage other people who are eager to read books to read and share by using these summaries. It is very valuable for me to share my thoughts about the book with people during the period I read and read on this website, which I created based on myself. I wanted to make a website that appeals to everyone, I hope you like it. I would be very happy if you share your comments, wishes and suggestions with me on the 'Contact' page.
