What is an eBook?

E-books are books that can be viewed and read on computers and other electronic devices, published digitally, containing text or pictures or both. Although it is referred to as electronic versions of books published in dictionaries, the number of books that are only published in electronic environment and never published is increasing. In general, any file that can be read on Ipad, Android tablets, Smart phones, book reader devices and computers can be called E-books.
The biggest advantage of e-Books is that you can carry hundreds of books with you. You can carry around 10,000 - 15,000 books with you with a 32 GB memory, either in your phone or tablet without carrying an additional device, or with special eBook reader devices that are very easy to carry. The closest e-book reading method to the real book experience is the e-book readers, which are produced for the purpose of reading e-books and do not contain a light source in the background and therefore do not tire the eyes more than a normal book. If you do not have an eBook reader, then you can also read books on your tablet or smart phone.

How to Gain the Habit of Reading Books?

1. Prefer to Start with Thin Books Let's admit that thick books can intimidate even people who have the habit of reading from time to time. Therefore, if you want to make reading a habit, we recommend that you choose thinner books that you will not be intimidated by.
2. Choose Books According to Your Interests
Reading on a subject you are not interested in can make you bored. Being bored prevents you from enjoying reading and thus from gaining a habit. You should choose the books that suit your interests.
3. Make a Reading List
After deciding which books to read thanks to reader comments, you can create a list of the books you want to read. This listing will help increase your motivation. 4. Determine Your Reading Space, Decide How Many Pages You Will Read Per Day You see many people reading books in different places. But in order to achieve this, you must first develop yourself in focusing while reading a book and gain a habit. It's a habit! So you should practice this regularly. And Most Importantly: Don't Give Up!

What are the Benefits of Reading Books?

Let's take a look at the benefits of reading before listing the suggestions that will help you gain the habit of reading books. Reading helps you learn new words, that is, improve your vocabulary. ... It helps you to approach an event from different perspectives and thus to be more successful in problem solving. It allows you to get to know different lives and views; strengthens your empathy skills. Reading a book is an action that requires patience and focus. After gaining the habit of reading books, you can be more successful in focusing on a problem or subject. You increase your knowledge and improve yourself. It allows you to reduce your stress level and relax.
Also, if you want to shape your career as a writer, reading a lot of books is among the steps you need to follow to become a writer!
